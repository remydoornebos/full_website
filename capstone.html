I always knew that I wanted to do a typeface for my final project but I had no idea it would turn into something like this. I wanted to combine some of the design philosophy I learned at DePaul in practice with letters. One of the more prominent things that I learned from design school is just how important negative space is in any design. So naturally I decided to make a typeface from the negative space between letters in an existing font.
